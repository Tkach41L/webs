How to get into YouTube trends?

The Trending section helps viewers see what's happening on YouTube and around the world. This section was created in order to present videos that will be of interest to a wide range of viewers. The appearance of some videos here is natural: new clips of famous performers and trailers for films are rapidly gaining popularity. Other videos, such as viral videos, end up in this section unexpectedly.
