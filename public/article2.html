Who is trending now?

The trends of each country differ from each other, but most often there are different artists and their new songs in the trends.

American YouTube

In America, the Roman Atwood Vlogs channel is more popularized, almost every video of it becomes the first in the trend. Other people filming vlogs or performers are also in the first places, at the moment Post Malone and Billie Eilish hold the first places.

Slovak YouTube

In Slovakia, 80% of the YouTube trend is occupied by songs of various genres. The remaining 20% are sports and bloggers. Mostly hockey or a blogger like StudioMoonTV.

Ukrainian YouTube

In Ukraine, the situation is similar. Most of the trends are music artists or blogs. Also in Ukraine, stand-up and automotive topics are popular. However, since Ukraine is a CIS warehouse, there are Russian and Belarusian videos in trends. The same situation is with Ukrainian videos in other countries.
